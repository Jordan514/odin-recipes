Lasagna

Description

This lasagna is a classic Italian dish that is a type of pasta. It's a layered casserole made with sheets of flat lasagna noodles, typically alternated with various ingredients such as ricotta or cottage cheese, mozzarella, Parmesan cheese, meat sauce (often containing ground beef or sausage), and tomato sauce. The layers are then baked until the noodles are cooked and the cheese is melted and bubbly.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Ground Beef
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Crushed Tomatoes
  • Tomato Paste
  • Olive Oil
  • Italian Herbs (Basil, Oregano, Thyme)
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Ricotta Cheese
  • Eggs
  • Parmesan Cheese
  • Mozzarella Cheese
  • Optional: Sautéed Mushrooms, Spinach, Italian Sausage

Steps

  1. Cook the lasagna noodles according to package instructions.
  2. In a pan, sauté chopped onion and garlic in olive oil.
  3. Add ground beef and cook until browned.
  4. Add crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, Italian herbs, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 15-20 minutes.
  5. In a bowl, mix ricotta cheese, eggs, and Parmesan cheese.
  6. Layer the lasagna in a baking dish: noodles, meat sauce, cheese filling, mozzarella. Repeat.
  7. Top with mozzarella cheese.
  8. Bake in a preheated oven at 375°F (190°C) for 30-35 minutes or until golden and bubbly.
  9. Let it cool slightly before serving.
